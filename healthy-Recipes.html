couscous Salad

This couscous salad is packed with protein and fresh vegetables.

  • 1 cup couscous
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, diced
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions: Cook the quinoa in water according to package instructions. Let it cool. Mix all ingredients together in a large bowl. Serve chilled.

Avocado Toast

A simple and delicious avocado toast recipe.

  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 2 slices whole grain bread
  • 1/2 lemon
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional toppings: cherry tomatoes, red pepper flakes, poached egg

Instructions: Toast the bread. Mash the avocado and spread it on the toast. Squeeze lemon juice over the avocado and season with salt and pepper. Add optional toppings if desired.
