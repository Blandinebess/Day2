Box Braids

Box braids are a popular protective hairstyle that involves sectioning the hair into small square-shaped parts and braiding them with synthetic hair extensions.

height="300" width="300" width="300" height="300"

Box braids are versatile and can be styled in various ways. They are low-maintenance and can last for several weeks with proper care.
